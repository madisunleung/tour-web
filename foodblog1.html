Guide to Vegetarian Food in HK

September 27, 2021

Looking for healthy options?
Here is the list of vegetarian and vegan restaurants for those who look to improve their diet

MANA!

Soho, Central

MANA! has pioneered the plant-based fast-casual market in Hong Kong since 2012, serving “fast slow food” and operating “zero waste” - whole-food, plant-based food, served fast, in an eco-friendly & responsible manner, packaged & ready to go.

Elephant Ground

Sai Ying Pun

Banana Toast & Smoothie

Vegan toast topped with sliced bananas and drizzled with agave. The smoothie is swapped for soy milk.

Elephant Grounds also present a consciously designed all-day menu featuring seasonal dishes that use premium Japanese ingredients and homemade pastries. Compared to MANA!, Elephant have much less vegetarian and vegan options. Plant-based milk is available for drinks.

14 SOUTH LANE

Shek Tong Tsui

14 South Lane promotes 'green eating' with an 80% vegetarian food menu. They proudly serve high-quality ingredients including seasonal vegetables from local organic farms, organic eggs and other organic ingredients. Every bite and sip are free from chemicals, msg, additives or refined ingredients. On top of that, the pancake menu changes every month so make sure to visit the restaurant monthly.
